Company Overview

CoreWeave, Inc. (ticker: CRWV) is a cloud infrastructure provider specializing in AI and high-performance computing (HPC). Founded in 2017 in Roseland, New Jersey, as Atlantic Crypto (initially focused on cryptocurrency mining), it pivoted to AI in 2019. It offers GPU-accelerated cloud services for AI workloads, positioning itself as the "#1 AI Cloud platform." CoreWeave went public on March 28, 2025, via IPO on NASDAQ, raising $1.5B at a $23B valuation. As of August 2025, it operates data centers globally, with recent expansions in the US and Europe.

Business Model and Services

CoreWeave provides Kubernetes-native cloud infrastructure optimized for AI, including:

  • Compute: GPU clusters for training and inference, with early access to NVIDIA GPUs like GB300 NVL72 and Blackwell RTX PRO 6000.
  • Storage and Networking: High-performance solutions for AI scale-out.
  • Managed Services: Tools like Slurm on Kubernetes, Tensorizer for inference optimization, and cluster health management.

Revenue is primarily from renting GPU resources to AI companies, with features like 10x faster inference spin-up and 96% cluster goodput. Key clients include OpenAI, MistralAI, IBM, Jane Street, Cohere, and Stability AI.
